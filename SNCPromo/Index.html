Conference Highlights
4 full days of thought-provoking discussions with workshop speakers on the cutting edge of where science, agriculture, food, nutrition, climate and human health meet.
Engagement with the Real Food Campaign partners and scientists with our "citizen science" lab booth! Bring your samples to contribute to the growing database and understand what's growing on your farm or garden
After 3 unforgettable days relax, network and mingle with speakers and attendees during the Saturday Night cocktail hour with a special address from BFA founder, Dan Kittredge.
This year, the conference will take place at the Southbridge Hotel & Conference Center in Southbridge, MA southwest of Boston, the weekend of November 15-17.
